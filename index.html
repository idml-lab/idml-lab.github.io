JeeHang is a KI PostDoc Fellow (2017- ) funded by KAIST Institute Research Felllowship at KAIST, KR, working with Prof. SangWan Lee in the Laboratory for Brain and Machine Intelligence. His research interests include reasoning and decision making based upon brain-inspired artificial intelligence.

He was a Research Associate working on ENLITEN (EP/K002724/1) at University of Bath, UK, from 2015 to 2016, where he was a winner of Recognising Execellence Scheme 2015 awarded by the university. He received his Ph.D. in AI from University of Bath in 2015, under the supervision of Dr. Julian A. Padget and Dr. Joanna J. Bryson. His Ph.D. research was supported by University Research Scholarship. Prior to his Ph.D. studies, he was a Senior Engineer at Samsung Electronics from 2005 to 2010, and Researcher at Hangul & Computer Inc from 2000 to 2005.
